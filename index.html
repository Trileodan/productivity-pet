Settings

Your monthly income

Set your monthly take-home, dated from when it started. Add a new dated figure whenever you get a pay rise or cut — earlier months keep the figure that applied at the time.

Categories & budgets

Set a £ amount for each category. Needs & Wants are spending limits (the bar turns red if you go over); Savings are minimum goals (bar stays green). Disable a category to hide it from the dashboard and pie chart — its past entries are kept and you can re-enable it any time.

Users & sign-in

Your data

Stored privately on this device only — nothing is uploaded. A second protected copy is kept automatically and restored if your browser ever clears its storage.

Install on your phone

iPhone: open this file in Safari, tap the Share button, then "Add to Home Screen".

Android: open in Chrome, tap the ⋮ menu, then "Add to Home screen".

It then opens full-screen like a normal app and works offline.
